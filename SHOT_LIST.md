# Jesse’s Pizza Company: Professional Photo Shot List

This document outlines the strategic, order-focused photo shot list for Jesse’s Pizza Company. These shots are designed to emphasize the "No Shortcuts" brand identity and drive conversion by focusing on texture, scale, and freshness.

---

## SECTION 1: HERO SHOTS (HIGH PRIORITY)
*Goal: Create an immediate physical craving.*

| Shot Name | Description | Angle | Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **The Cheese Pull** | A hand lifting a slice of the Jalapeño Popper Pizza from the tray. Macro focus on stretching cheese. | 45° Side View | Hero Background / Landing Page Top |
| **The Oven Exit** | 18" Jumbo Pizza being pulled out of the stone oven with a metal peel. Steam should be visible. | Eye-Level | "Why Us" Section / Our Story |
| **The "Big Pie" Overhead** | A perfectly centered top-down of a Pepperoni Pizza on a metal tray. | 90° Flat Lay | Banner / Section Break |

---

## SECTION 2: BEST SELLER SHOTS
*Goal: Show the customer exactly what they are getting.*

*   **Jalapeño Popper Pizza**: Focus on the cream cheese dollops and charred pepper edges.
*   **Chicken Bacon Ranch**: Focus on the ranch drizzle and crispy bacon bits.
*   **Pepperoni Pizza**: Focus on "cupping" pepperoni with visible oil droplets.
*   **18" Jumbo Pizza**: Shot next to a standard item (like a soda can) for scale.

**Style Rules:**
- Angle: 30° Isometric (close-up slices).
- Visible toppings: Ensure every key ingredient is identifiable.
- Placement: Individual Menu Item thumbnails in the ordering system.

---

## SECTION 3: ACTION SHOTS
*Goal: Prove everything is made fresh and human-led.*

| Capture | Purpose | Angle |

| **The Slice** | Pizza rocker blade coming down through a fresh crust. | Dynamic / POV |
| **The Sauce Swirl** | Ladle spreading red sauce in a perfect spiral on raw dough. | Overhead |

## SECTION 4: SIDE ITEMS
*Goal: Make the "extra" feel essential.*

*   **Breadsticks**: Piled high in a basket with a small bowl of marinara dipping.
*   **Wings**: Buffalo wings with blue cheese/ranch, showing the "crisp" of the skin.
*   **Cold Drinks**: A 2L bottle or fountain cup with heavy condensation (looking ice-cold).

**Style Rules:**
- Angle: Low Angle (Heroic) to make the sides look substantial.
- Tone: Hot, fresh, and ready to eat.
- Placement: Cart Upsell / Sides Category.

## SECTION 5: LOCATION SHOTS
*Goal: Make the pickup process feel familiar and easy.*

*   **The Exterior**: The Borger/Fritch storefront with the lights on at dusk.
*   **The Counter**: A clean pickup counter with a "Jesse's Pizza" box ready to go.

---

## SECTION 6: STYLE & COMPOSITION RULES

### Lighting Recommendations
- **Avoid Flat Light**: Do not use generic overhead "buffet" lighting.
- **Use Side-Lighting**: This creates shadows that emphasize the texture of the crust and the topography of the toppings.
- **Tone**: Warm and Rich. Reds should be deep (Burnt Red), and crusts should be golden-brown.

### Composition Rules
- **No Stock Photos**: Authenticity is part of the brand.
- **Tight Crops**: Focus on food texture; eliminate empty space.
- **Real Propping**: Use metal baking sheets, parchment paper, or dark wooden cutting boards. No white tablecloths.
- **No Over-Editing**: Keep it looking like real food for real people.
